# 🔢 Numerology Calculator

A dark, mystical numerology app built with React + Vite.

Enter your full birth name and date of birth to reveal your three core numbers based on the Pythagorean system.

## ✦ What it calculates

- **Life Path** — your life's purpose (from your birthdate)
- **Expression** — your natural talents (from all letters in your name)
- **Soul Urge** — your inner desires (from the vowels in your name)
